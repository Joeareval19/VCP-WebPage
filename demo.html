10

Page transition — title dip

Clicking a nav tab dips to black (--dur-dip-in), the destination's serif title breathes alone for a beat, then a slow fade-away (--dur-dip-out) reveals the page. The real navigation loads hidden behind the black. Honors prefers-reduced-motion; anchors and external links are unaffected. Component: html.vcp-dip in components.css + js/page-dip.js.

11

Loading indicator — Penrose quicksilver

Clicking an internal link outside the top nav populates a Penrose tri-bar over a veil (--scrim); the sheen sweeps while navigation fires behind it, then the destination blurs into focus as the veil lifts. The top nav keeps the title dip; the two never fire together. Under prefers-reduced-motion navigation is native and immediate. Component: .vcp-loader in components.css + js/page-loader.js.
