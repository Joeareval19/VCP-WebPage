# Product

Strategic design context for VCP-WebPage — read by design tooling (impeccable) and agents before any UI work. Visual law lives in DESIGN.md; this file answers who/what/why.

## Register

brand

## Users

Visitors arriving cold from a link — potential clients, collaborators, and readers of the research. Their job: understand what VCP is and who Jose is in one calm read, then decide whether to go deeper into a wing (Research / Projects / Library). Secondary users: Jose and the agent fleet, who inherit this context on every build.

## Product Purpose

The public, open repository of Vegas Consulting Partners' work — projects, research topics, and white papers, published as they are made. The work itself is the record. Success on the landing page: a visitor gets the shape of the site in one unhurried read and clicks into a wing.

## Brand Personality

**Quiet confidence** — calm · deliberate · inviting. Dark metallic material (graphite, liquid glass, silver sheen) carried with a human voice: warm VCP register site-wide, first person where Jose appears. The page breathes; nothing performs. It should feel like a person with taste, not a firm with a deck.

## Anti-references

- **Consulting-firm brochure**: service grids, credential walls, stock-handshake energy, "we deliver value" copy. The formality this brand is actively removing.
- **SaaS landing template**: hero metric + feature-card grids + logo wall + gradient CTA. The default AI landing-page shape.

## Design Principles

1. **Less, but better.** Fewer moments, each earning its place; whitespace is a material, not leftover space.
2. **The sheen is precious.** Metallic accents at key moments only — overuse kills the class (DESIGN.md rule 3).
3. **Invite, don't perform.** Composition and copy make reading feel effortless: snapshot first, depth one click away.
4. **A person lives here.** The company never fully hides Jose; first person is allowed and welcome where he speaks.
5. **Tokens are law.** Every visual decision resolves to the design system (`css/tokens.css`, `css/components.css`, `demo.html`).

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA: body text ≥4.5:1 contrast (the faintest text token is decorative-only, never sentences), visible focus rings, `prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives for all motion, semantic landmarks, keyboard-usable interactions.
